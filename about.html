ABOUT CHAMELEON

"We envision a world where

all children enjoy learning"

Discovering Curiousity

Curiosity is a powerful force that drives exploration, learning, and discovery. With our game, you'll have the opportunity to explore new topics and expand your knowledge with new play cards every month! Are the questions a little too hard this month, and did you not manage to beat your dad? Don’t worry, next month you will have new changes and maybe you will be able to beat him then!

Innovation

At Chameleon, we believe in the power of innovation to create truly magical experiences for children. Our game is designed to keep kids engaged and entertained month after month, with new challenges, adventures, and surprises around every corner. Every month, our team of expert game designers and child development specialists creates a brand-new part of the game that is specifically tailored to children's needs and interests. With our constantly changing games, your child will always have something new and exciting to discover - and who knows what amazing things we'll come up with!

Rising Fun

Fun is a something we highly praise at chameleon. Fun is important in the lives of not only childeren but also by parents. The game can therefore be played between parents and childeren but also between childeren and their friends. So the childeren can play the game with different people adding new dimensions for them and see what they like the most. Also every month a new set of cards is released to play the game in a different way. This make the game very easily re-playable and so keeping the fun in the game. The child can experience the game in a lot of different ways but what always keeps the same is the fun.
